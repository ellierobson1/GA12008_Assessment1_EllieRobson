Key Policies

  • Annual Leave: Request via manager approval. Recommended notice: At least 2 weeks.
  • Sickness: Notify your manager by 9:30am. A fit note may be required for extended absence periods.
  • Data Protection: Only access employee data required for your role. Do not share personal data without authorisation of a manager.
  • Equal Opportunities: We provide a respectful workplace. Please report concerns to HR.
